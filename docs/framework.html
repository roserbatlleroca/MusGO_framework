MusGO Framework: Detailed Criteria

We here describe with complete detail the Music-Generative Open AI (MusGO) framework: a community-driven framework for assessing openness in music-generative AI models. This depicted criteria is linked to the research article 'MusGO: A Community-Driven Framework for Assessing Openness in Music-Generative AI', which is currently under review.
